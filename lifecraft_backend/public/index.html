Destroy boredom, gain XP

Bored with nothing to do? Lifecraft helps you find activities near you so you can attend them!

Get started »

Receive Quest

A quest gives you, for the day, a series of activities near you to attend and quell your boredom.

Check In

Once you attend an event or activity, check in and we'll mark it off of your quest.

Level Up

For each activity you attend, you get experience points. Once you receive enough XP, you level up!
